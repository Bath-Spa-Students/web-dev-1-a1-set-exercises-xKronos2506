Mom's Air Fryer Chicken

An everyday to-go air fryer chicken recipe.
Aside from that it is healthy, it as well retains its deliciousness, all without using a huge amount of oil to cook it

Ingredients:

Spices:

  • 1 teaspoon Paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon Smoked Paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon Salt
  • 11/2 teaspoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch

Chicken:

  • 2 teaspoons avocado oil
  • 1 pound boneless skinless chic ken breasts

Instruction:

  1. Cut the chicken into thin pieces: Cut each chicken breast into thin, flat pieces (not bite-sized but something that looks like a thin chicken breast).
  2. Pat the chicken breasts dry if they have a lot of moisture on them
  3. Coat the chicken with spices: Toss the spices together so theyâ€™re mixed well; sprinkle mixture over the chicken pieces in a bowl. Add the oil and toss . Thoroughly coat the chicken.
  4. Air fry: Preheat the air fryer. Place the chicken on the air fryer basket / grate in an even layer, leaving a bit of space for air to circulate around. Air fry the chicken at 415 degrees for 10 minutes.
